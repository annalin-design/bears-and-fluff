ingredients:
- 2-3 Chicken Breasts
- 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 4 Garlic Cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp Honey
- 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
- 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
---

## Instructions
1. Chop chicken into pieces
2. Mix in marinade in a bowl, add chicken
3. Marinade chicken overnight
4. Stir fry when ready to eat
